Watch Cristiano Ronaldo endorse bizarre Japanese product 'Facial Fitness Pao'

Aug 6 2014

Real Madrid star Cristiano Ronaldo has endorsed several products throughout his career, however, the Facial Fitness Pao could just be the weirdest of them all.

During his visit to Japan last month, Ronaldo shot an ad for the fitness product that helps people work on their facial muscles, giving them a youthful smile – well that is what the company claims.

No wonder you don’t see Ronaldo using that product himself in the video because even he realises that this product is just weird, and endorsing it probably was the worst decision he had ever made.
